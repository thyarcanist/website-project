Summary

On a planet known as Panchaea, two factions are fighting to survive: The Kryukians and the Coalition. Aurelia Octavian is the daughter of Olivia, the Warden, Octavian II. She must navigate through political intrigue and war to discover who she really is and find her route to redemption. The path is rocky and uncertain but for she wishes to succeed and plant seeds of kindness throughout the land-- the Wights, dark eldritch horrors shock and awe settlers-- the lands of Ard Pzchif are ripe for the harvest and the Coalition squabbles with territory woes.
